Sami and The Giant

Story and Game Time

The Bed

Sammy hid his panic and asked the giant if he had a free bed which he can sleep on for the night . Sammy was impressed by the giant's welcome.

He showed him the room which had a small bed relatively to the giant's size which made Sammy anxious.

The Anxiety

Sammy turned off the light to sleep but was very afraid of the Giant not knowing his real intentions which seemed too nice to be true!

So he could'nt cope with his doubts so he started of thinking about hiding in the room like under the bed.

2 Choices where available at that moment , sleep normally or hide under the bed.
