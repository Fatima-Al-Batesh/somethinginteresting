Sami and The Giant

Story and Game Time

Run Choice

Sammy didn't even introduce him self he ran as fast as the wind seeking to save his life and not being the giant's dinner !

Sammy began to feel the pain in his legs , as well as doubt filled his mind about the intentions of the giant "did he want to do me any harm?". Sammy's body as well as mind needed some rest so he lit a campfire to get warmer . He spent the night around the camp thinking it's not such a lucky day is it ?

The Sun Rise

The sun came back which gave Sammy a sense of hope !

He felt a sore in his throat and came to his senses that he is thirsty a lot!

He headed forward where he reached a pond and thoughts of drinking from it took place in his mind.

2 Choices where available at that moment , drinking from the pond or not drinking.
