Sami and The Giant

Story and Game Time

The Beginning

Sami , a poor kid which has no parents living in a village turned 16. He decided that he needs to move out of this village since he can't see a worthy future here!

He started packing and preparing for his long and hopefully great and non-problematic trip!

He decided to head towards a forest since tails where told in his village that behind this forest miracles happened!

The Trip

It was a sunny day , Samy decided to start his trip at this day since he felt lucky as soon as he woke up !

Everything was going right , Samy did really believe it's his lucky day

Night came up , darkness nothing can be seen except a small house which Samy thought he can rest in by asking help from the habitants.

He approached the small house and it was very creepy yet very wierd looking , so he thought to himself : 'let's knock on the door and give it a try!'.As he knocked , the door went wide open and a big figure appeared and after seconds Samy realized the habitant is a Giant!

2 Choices where available at that moment , run or ask for a bed.
